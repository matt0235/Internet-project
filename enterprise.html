ENTERPRISE SOFTWARE

Enterprise software is like a big toolbox for large businesses. It includes different computer programs that help with things like planning, managing customers, and handling employees.
This type of software is designed specifically for big companies, providing one central place for managing data and making important decisions.

 

PAYMENT SYSTEMS

Payment systems are like the money managers of enterprise software. They help businesses handle their finances smoothly by taking care of payments and transactions.
Payment systems like Paypal™ make sure that different types of payments, like credit cards or online transfers, are processed securely.

Paypal

Like mentioned before, Paypal is a popular online payment platform that makes money transactions easy for people and businesses. It started in 1998 and allows users to securely send and receive money, buy things online, and set up regular payments. Users connect their bank accounts or cards to their PayPal accounts, making payments straightforward without sharing private financial details with sellers.

Stripe

Stripe is a popular tool that helps businesses handle online payments. It started in 2010 and lets companies accept different types of online payments, making things simple for both businesses and their customers.
What makes Stripe stand out is its easy-to-use design and tools that developers find helpful. This makes it straightforward for businesses to set up secure and smooth payment processing on their websites or apps.

 

WEB CUSTOMER RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT SOFTWARE

Web Customer Relationship Management (CRM) software is a digital tool designed to help businesses manage and improve their interactions with customers online.
Accessed through web browsers, it acts as a central hub for storing customer info, tracking communication, and making sales and support processes smoother.

Some examples of great CRM services are:

Salesforce
Hubspot
Microsoft Dynamics 365
Insightly

SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT SYSTEMS

Supply Chain Management (SCM) is all about making sure things run smoothly when creating and delivering products. It involves coordinating and improving processes from making the product to getting it to the customer.

Some of the more popular SCM options are:

  • SAP Integrated Business Planning (IBP)
  • Oracle SCM Cloud
  • Kinaxis RapidResponse
