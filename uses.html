Communication

Communication is a Vital part of our day to day life,
Accross the web there are hundreds of forms of communication to do a wide variety of things.
You can contact friends, family, co workers, co-ordinate meetings and much more.

Some of the more popular Websites are the following:

Whatsapp

Messaging service founded by Jan Koum and Brian Acton, whatsapp is by far one of the most popular forms of communication.
Whatsapp can be used to message anyone also on the service and make group chats with up to 1024 people.

Zoom

Founded by Eric Yuan in 2019, Zoom is a service mainly used for Online web-meetings and conferance calls.
Zoom rose to popularity during the COVID-19 Pandemic due to the populations inability to meet in person.
Zoom Remains to this day, a very useful tool for large scale meetings.

Slack

Slack is a service mainly used for team-collaborating and file sharing amongst people in the workplace.
Slack can be a very useful tool for employers to keep their employees up to date on deadlines, events and meetings in a work envoirment.

Research

Students Need reliable places to get good information on topics.
Often search engines can be wrong or poorly sourced leading to frustration and poor work flow.
Some of these examples can really help students be more productive and help meet deadlines in college/school.

Google Scholar

Google Scholar is Search engine designed specifically for students.
Released to the public in 2014, It serves as a more reliable search engine for academic information prioritising more studys, research papers and other academic sources in the search results.
Using this service as oppose to conventional search engines relieves alot of the frustration for a student.

Science.gov

Science.gov is a website owned and operated by science specialists all around the world.
it is the go-to website for anything science related as it searches from 60 databases, 200 million pages of journals research papers, etc and 2200 websites. There is a variety of filters to ensure you find just what you are looking for.

Google books

Google books is a tool used to search from thousands of books.
These books can be:

  1. Old
  2. New
  3. Popular
  4. Informative

When you find a book you like, you can look through the pages and even order a hard copy

Virtual Learning Resources Center

(VLRC) is an index of acedemic websites full of well sourced and valid information.
Users can choose from thousands of these websites put together by teachers, professors and doctors around the globe.
All the information stored on these websites are up to date and are regulated to make sure that there are no errors.

Collaboration

Collaboration is essential for efficient work flow and teamwork in an office envoirment and many other projects whether it be for just yourself, or others.
These following services allow easy-to-use and Excellent features to help any team achieve a goal.

Todoist

Todoist is a user-friendly task management app designed to help people stay organized and focused on their goals. With its simple interface, users can effortlessly create and manage tasks. The app offers features like project organization, due dates, priority levels, and collaboration tools, catering to both personal and professional needs.

Miro

Miro is a collaborative online platform designed to facilitate teamwork, creativity, and visual collaboration among remote teams. Formerly known as RealtimeBoard, Miro provides a virtual whiteboard experience that allows users to brainstorm ideas, create mind maps, and collaborate on various projects in real-time.

eCommerce

E-commerce, or electronic commerce, is the online buying and selling of goods and services. It transcends geographical limitations, enabling businesses and consumers to conduct transactions in a digital space.

Shopify

Shopify is a leading e-commerce platform that empowers businesses to establish and manage their online stores with ease.
Launched in 2006, Shopify offers a user-friendly interface, allowing entrepreneurs and established brands to create customizable digital storefronts without extensive technical expertise.

Wix

Wix is a popular cloud-based website development platform that empowers individuals and businesses to create stunning and functional websites without the need for extensive coding knowledge.
Launched in 2006, Wix offers a user-friendly drag-and-drop interface, allowing users to easily customize templates and design their own websites with a variety of their own assets.

Squarespace

Alot like wix, squarespace is a user-friendly tool for creating websites without the need for advanced tech skills. It started in 2004 and is known for its stylish templates that work well for various types of websites.
